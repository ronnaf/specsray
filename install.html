Specsray - Bookmarklet Install

Drag the button below to your bookmarks bar. Click it on any page to toggle the measurement overlay; click it again (or the panel's close button) to remove it.

Specsray
  1. Show your bookmarks bar (Ctrl/Cmd+Shift+B in Firefox).
  2. Drag the black button above into the bookmarks bar.
  3. Visit any page and click the bookmark to toggle the overlay.

Sites with a strict Content-Security-Policy may block bookmarklets from running; there is no workaround for that from the bookmarklet side.
